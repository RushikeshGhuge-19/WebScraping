This 2019 BMW X5 M50d offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, boasting a powerful 3.0L diesel engine and a sophisticated four wheel drive system. As a Euro 6 compliant vehicle, it meets stringent emission standards. This X5 comes equipped with a host of desirable features, including Rear Seat Entertainment for passenger enjoyment, Adaptive 2-Axle Air Suspension for a smooth ride, and the flexibility of Third Row Seating. The Sky Lounge Panoramic Glass Sunroof, which includes an opening function, floods the cabin with natural light, while the Comfort Package adds convenience with Keyless Entry, heated seats, and memory seat functions. Experience impressive acceleration with this X5 M50d, capable of reaching 0-62mph in just 5.2 seconds, a testament to its potent powertrain. This performance, combined with its substantial 2700 kg towing weight, positions it as a highly capable vehicle for a variety of needs. The generous boot space further enhances its practicality, making it suitable for family adventures or transporting larger items. These highlights demonstrate how this particular BMW X5 M50d stands out from other vehicles in its class, offering a superior combination of speed, comfort, and utility.
